# Find Movies to Watch

**Discover what to watch — and where to watch it.**

Find Movies helps you browse, discover, and track movies across popular streaming services. Build your watchlist, check where movies are available on Netflix, Prime Video, or Apple TV, and keep everything in sync across all your Apple devices.

## Key Features

### **Browse & Discover**
Explore popular movies, top-rated films, and full genre libraries. Whether you're in the mood for action, drama, or a quiet documentary, Find Movies has you covered with up-to-date content powered by The Movie Database.

### **Streaming Availability**
See at a glance where a movie is available right now — Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, or Apple TV. Tap through directly to the streaming app to start watching immediately.

### **Watchlist**
Save movies you want to watch with a single tap. Your watchlist syncs privately across all your devices via iCloud — start browsing on iPhone, pick up on iPad, or check it on Apple TV.

### **Cast & Crew**
Dive into the details of any movie: cast, crew, genres, ratings, and similar titles. Tap on any cast member to browse their other work.

### **Home Screen Widget**
The WatchNext widget puts your next movie right on the home screen — poster, title, and where to watch it — without opening the app.

### **Multi-Platform**
Find Movies runs natively on iPhone, iPad, Apple TV, and Mac. Each platform has a layout optimized for that screen and input method.

## Private by Design

Your watchlist lives in your own private iCloud container. Find Movies does not collect or transmit any personal information. Movie data comes anonymously from The Movie Database API.
