# YATTA: Time Tracking on Toggl

**Your Toggl time tracking, right from your pocket.**

YATTA connects directly to your Toggl Track account, giving you a fast and focused way to track time on iOS and macOS. Start and stop timers, browse your history, and keep your projects on track — all without opening a browser.

## Key Features

### **Timer Control**
Start and stop time entries with a tap. Quickly duplicate a previous entry to resume work, or begin a fresh timer from scratch. The running timer is always visible so you never lose track of what you're working on.

### **Time Entries**
Browse your full time entry history organized by date. Navigate between days with a calendar overlay or jump straight to today. Pull to refresh keeps your data up to date, and a context menu lets you edit or delete any entry quickly.

### **Projects**
Organize your work across projects. Assign projects to time entries, browse all entries for a specific project, and see your project history going back a full year with automatic pagination.

### **iOS Widget**
Glance at your running timer right from your home screen or lock screen. The widget shows the current project, its color, and elapsed time — and lets you stop the timer without opening the app.

### **Multiple Accounts & Workspaces**
Manage multiple Toggl accounts and workspaces from one place. Switch between them and keep personal and professional time clearly separated.

## Built for iPhone, iPad, and Mac

YATTA is a native app on every platform:
- Full iPhone and iPad support with an interface designed for quick timer control
- iOS home screen widget
